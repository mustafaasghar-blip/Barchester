About the House of Barchester

Al Madrassa al Barchesteriya was established to preserve and transmit authentic Islamic knowledge upon the understanding of the righteous predecessors. Our name derives from the noble House of Barchester, a symbol of educational heritage.

Location: Virtual Madrasa - Accessible worldwide

Methodology: Salafi - Following Qur'an & Sunnah upon the understanding of the Salaf
